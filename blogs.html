Blog Posts

Here you'll find my collection of blog posts. I began writing these the summer before I started grad school, and aim to upload once a month (on the first Sunday). I focuse primarily on topics in mathematics, but integrate my own experiences and philosophies in each post.

These posts are inteded for a broad audience, and I typically try to format these so that an advanced high school student can understand most, if not all, of what I write. As such, this blog is not formal, nor does it contain research-level mathematics.
